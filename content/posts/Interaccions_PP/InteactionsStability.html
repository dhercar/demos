Context

This post is inspired by an assignment I did with Yanis Zentner during my MS in Ecology. We were ask to submit an abstract and prepare a talk pretending we were in a scientific congress about biotic interactions. Inspired by McCann (2000) , we decided to investigate about the relationship between interactions and ecosystem stability. We were supposed to do some bibliographic research, which we did, but I couldn’t stand the chance of replicating and manipulating the simulations I was reading about.

Introduction

When a natural ecosystem is free from perturbation it reaches a stable state where every species abundance stays the same… or does it???
